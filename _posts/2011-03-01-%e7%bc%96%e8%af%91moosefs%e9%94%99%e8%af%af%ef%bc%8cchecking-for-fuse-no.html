fuse安装
[cc lang="bash"]./configure --prefix=/opt/fuse --mandir=/usr/share/man
make && make install[/cc]

编译moosefs
[cc lang="bash"]./configure --prefix=/opt/mfs --disable-mfsmaster --disable-mfschunkserver[/cc]
提示错误，checking for FUSE... no

解决：
执行命令：
[cc lang="bash"]export PKG_CONFIG_PATH=/opt/fuse/lib/pkgconfig:$PKG_CONFIG_PATH[/cc]
完后再重新编译
make clean
./configure
